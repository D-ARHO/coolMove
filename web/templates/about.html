About Kold Tracker

Kold Tracker is dedicated to providing real-time cold chain monitoring solutions using low-cost, reliable ESP32 and GPS technology.

Our Mission

To ensure the safety and integrity of perishable goods by offering precise, real-time location and temperature data, accessible from any web-enabled device.

Technology Stack

  • **Device:** ESP32, NEO-6M GPS, DS18B20 Temperature Sensor.
  • **Firmware:** PlatformIO (C++) for reliable, resource-efficient data transmission.
  • **Web Backend:** Flask (Python) with PostgreSQL for secure data storage.
